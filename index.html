Course Outcomes

CO Course Outcome Bloom's Level
CO1 Understand the internetworking design in terms of protocol stack and the role of various application layer protocols. K2
CO2 Illustrate the functions of the transport layer from connectionless and connection-oriented perspectives. K3
CO3 Identify how the network layer achieves host-to-host connectivity and caters to the diverse service requirements of host applications. K3
CO4 Explain the nuances of the data link layer design and demonstrate the various data link layer protocols. K3
CO5 Describe the fundamental characteristics of the physical layer and understand how the physical layer supports the functionalities of the top layers. K2
Bloom's Knowledge Levels:
K1 - Remember K2 - Understand K3 - Apply K4 - Analyse K5 - Evaluate K6 - Create
